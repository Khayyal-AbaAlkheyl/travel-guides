⭐ Recommendation for دليلك
This itinerary is designed so that travelers experience Italy's three distinct personalities:
Milan – Fashion, design, and modern Italy.
Northern Italy – Lakes, canals, and charming historic towns.
Central Italy – Renaissance art, Tuscany's landscapes, and the ancient grandeur of Rome.
For the guide's visual style, use warm terracotta tones, olive green accents, and cream backgrounds, with large photography of alpine lakes, Venetian canals, Tuscan vineyards, and Rome's iconic landmarks. Each destination should feel visually distinct while maintaining a consistent premium editorial style.
Bosnia & Herzegovina is one of the best destinations for GCC travelers, but many itineraries make a mistake: they spend too much time in Sarajevo. The country's real beauty is in its mountains, rivers, waterfalls, and Ottoman towns.
For دليلك, I recommend a 7-day road trip. Renting a car is ideal, but travelers can also hire a private driver or join day tours.

✨ Why this itinerary is better than typical Azerbaijan guides
Most travel guides spend 5–6 days in Baku, which quickly becomes repetitive. This itinerary gives travelers the full diversity of Azerbaijan by combining:
Modern architecture in Baku.
Ancient Silk Road heritage in Lahij and Sheki.
Mountain landscapes in Gabala and Guba.
High-altitude village life in Khinalug.
Natural wonders like Gobustan, mud volcanoes, waterfalls, forests, and lakes.
For دليلك, this approach creates a memorable journey where each day feels different. It also makes the guide visually richer, with changing landscapes and experiences, instead of repeating the same urban setting throughout the trip.
